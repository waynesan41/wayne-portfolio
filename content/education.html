Computer Science Degree

 During the COVID-19 pandemic, I had to stop attending school due to personal and financial challenges.
 Despite not pursuing a formal degree, I remained dedicated to my passion for coding and technology while working a full-time job.

My Path

 I turned to online resources like Udemy, Linkedin Learning, YouTube, and textbooks to teach myself programming, frameworks, and software engineering concepts.

 Through this self-driven education, I built projects to deepen my understanding of software development, showcasing my adaptability, and dedication to any new technology.

 A major disadvantage of being self-taught is missing out on the networking and career opportunities that school offers, such as coaching events and professional networking.
